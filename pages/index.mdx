# Jimuel Palaca

He started programming in his 3rd-year high school, and got his BSc degree in **Information and Communication Technology** from Bulacan State University. Jim works now as a **software engineer** in [Tappollo Media, LLC](https://www.tappollo.com/), he has been contributing to the **open-source** community since 2019. Jim also enjoys playing video games, chess, photography, and MMA.
